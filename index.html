"Space Changer Demo" is the demo where you can enter in a comma separated list of words / phrases and a comma separated list of symbols, and it will output a list of those words with their spaces changed to no spaces, and any symbols entered in the list.

"Profanity Demo" is the demo where you can enter in a word with a bunch of letters, symbols, and numbers, and it will translate it. Like "H3110 J@va$cr!p+" -> "Hello JavaScript".
